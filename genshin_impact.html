Game Description

Genshin Impact is an open-world RPG where you can play as multiple characters.

How To Play

PVE, multiplayer and singleplayer
WASD to move, ESC to open Paimon Menu
Each character has three attacks: Normal,(use right side of mouse) Elemental Skill (press E)
and Elemental Burst (press Q)
To get characters, you use Primogems in the Wishing screen. Every 90 pulls, you are guaranteed a 5-star character or weapon.
